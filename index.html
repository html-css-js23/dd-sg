How to Use the Ledger

Colors & Lines:

  • Blue: UP Trains / UP Lines (Travelling Left towards Masonfield)
  • Green: DOWN Trains / DOWN Lines (Travelling Right towards Ashburn)
  • Purple: Branch Lines (Gleethrop End & Cosdale Harbour)
  • Red Pulse: Train On Line (Occupied Track Section)

Mechanics:

  • Moving: Click and drag the train card to any platform or block line drop zone.
  • Interlocks: The system enforces Absolute and Tokenless Block rules. It will block you from dragging a train into a track section that is already occupied.
  • Removing: Drag a train to the "END OF LINE" column to permanently despawn it.
  • Saving: The board automatically saves your trains to your browser. You can safely refresh the page without losing track of your trains.
